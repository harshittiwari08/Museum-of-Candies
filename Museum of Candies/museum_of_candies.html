A Sweet Adventure for All Ages

Step into a world of color, creativity, and confections! The Museum of Candies is not just a museum — it’s an immersive experience where candy lovers of all ages can relive childhood memories, discover the history of sweets, and indulge in a little sugar-coated fun.

About the Museum

At the Museum of Candies, we believe every treat tells a story. From ancient honey-based confections to modern-day marvels like cotton candy clouds and gummy bear tunnels, our interactive exhibits take you on a journey through time, taste, and imagination.

What You'll Discover

  • Candy Lab: Watch how your favorite treats are made — and even create your own!
  • Sweet History Tunnel: Explore the evolution of candies across centuries and continents.
  • Giant Candy Land: Walk through oversized lollipops, marshmallow mountains, and chocolate rivers.
